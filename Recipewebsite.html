Angela's Recipe for the best Cinnamon rolls

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • 3/4 cup warm milk
  • 21/4 teaspoons yeast
  • 1/4 cup Granulated sugar
  • 1 egg plus 1 egg yolk
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 3 cups bread flour

For the Filling:

  • 2/3 cup dark brown sugar
  • 11/2 tablespoons ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup butter

Instructions

  1. Mix the milk with the yeast,sugar,eggs
  2. Melt the butter and add to the mixture
  3. Add in the flour and mix until combined into a dough
  4. Knead the dough for 5 minutes
  5. Transfer the dough into a large bowl and cover with plastic wrap
  6. After dough has doubled in size roll it out into a large rectangle sheet
  7. Cut the sheet into smaller rectangles
  8. Fill in with your fav filling
  9. roll the dough the filling
  10. Set the roll in oven for around 15 minutes at 270 degree celsius
